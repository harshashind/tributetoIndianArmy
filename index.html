Tribute To Our National Heroes

Founded: 1 April 1895

This page is Tribute to the Bravest Indian Army


“The veterans of our military services have put their lives on the line to protect the freedoms that we enjoy.
They have dedicated their lives to their country and deserve to be recognized for their commitment.”
-Judd Gregg


Love for our mother India creates the firm base of the Indian army. He sacrifices his life for the protection of the nation’s pride. Every soldier is a true patriot and is always ready to lay down his life in the service of motherland. Our nation’s protection and safety are totally dependent on our soldiers. Indian Army not only fights in the war but also take care of the rescue operations when natural calamities occur like cyclones, earth-quakes. We must feel the pain of the great sacrifice which our Indian Army is doing every day to keeping our country, our people together and safe. Thus, we all salute our Indian heroes and pay homage to the supreme sacrifice which our army brothers are doing every day.


Being an Indian army officer, one is meant to subsume their persona under a very demanding but explicit code which states that, “THE SAFETY, HONOUR AND WELFARE OF YOUR COUNTRY COME FIRST, ALWAYS AND EVERY TIME. THE HONOUR, WELFARE AND COMFORT OF THE MEN YOU COMMAND COME NEXT. YOUR OWN EASE, COMFORT AND SAFETY COME LAST, ALWAYS AND EVERY TIME.” For them, their service to the country is more important than anything else. They are undoubtedly the real superheroes of our nation.



In the realm of valor and might,
Where heroes shine like stars at night,
A tribute to those who stand tall,
The guardians of our nation's call.

From fields of war to peaceful shores,
They keep us safe, our hearts they soar
A debt of gratitude we owe,
For their sacrifices, we bestow.
